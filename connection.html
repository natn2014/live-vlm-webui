live-vlm-webui · connection architecture

How a video frame gets from your camera to a sentence on screen

One browser tab, one Python process on the DGX Spark, and a VLM backend it can swap out live. Three channels do the work: HTTPS for the page, WebRTC for video, WSS for everything the server wants to tell you about it.
